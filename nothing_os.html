History of Nothing

Nothing OS

Nothing OS, developed by Nothing, a tech company founded by Carl Pei, debuted with the Nothing Phone (1) in 2022 as a minimalist Android-based system. Its "dot matrix" design, inspired by retro tech, emphasizes simplicity, smooth performance, and a bloatware-free experience, integrating features like the Glyph Interface for LED notifications. While praised for its unique aesthetic, early versions lacked some advanced features compared to rivals like Samsung’s One UI.

Nothing OS has evolved through user-focused updates, with Nothing OS 2.0 (2023) introducing enhanced widgets, glyph features, and efficiency improvements. By 2025, Nothing OS 3.0, built on Android 14, offers AI personalization, better multitasking, and ecosystem integration with Nothing’s wearables, establishing it as a polished, distinctive Android skin.

Home screen

Nothing OS homescreen customization enables users to personalize their layout with monochromatic icon packs, adjustable app grids, and a variety of widgets, all styled in its distinctive dot-matrix design. It offers a minimalist, cohesive aesthetic that can be tailored with third-party icon packs for added flexibility.

Lockscreen

Nothing OS lockscreen customization offers users the ability to personalize with unique clock styles, functional widgets, and quick-access app shortcuts, all styled in its signature monochromatic and dot-matrix aesthetic. You can tailor notification visibility and select wallpapers to create a clean, minimalist lockscreen experience.

Quick settings

Nothing OS Quick Settings customization allows users to rearrange tiles for easy access to key functions and add or remove toggles to suit their needs. Its minimalist design, with a signature monochromatic and dot-matrix aesthetic, ensures a clean, visually distinct panel.

Nothing OS 1.0

Nothing OS 1.0 launched with the Nothing Phone (1) in 2022, focusing on a clean, minimalist Android experience with zero bloatware. Its signature feature was a unified dot-matrix visual language used for all design elements, and it was the software engine that drove the unique Glyph Interface light patterns on the back of the phone.

Nothing OS 2.0

Nothing OS 2.0, launched in 2023 with the Nothing Phone (2), refines its minimalist Android design with enhanced widgets, new Glyph Interface features, and better power efficiency. It offers a smooth, bloatware-free experience with a polished "dot matrix" aesthetic.

Nothing OS 3.0

Nothing OS 3.0, launched in 2025 on Android 14, enhances its minimalist design with AI personalization, smoother animations, and better multitasking. It integrates with Nothing’s wearables, adds battery health features, and maintains a bloatware-free "dot matrix" interface.

Nothing OS 4.0

Nothing OS 4.0, released in open beta in September 2025 on Android 16, refines its minimalist design with new lock screen clocks, circular icons, and AI-powered "Essential Apps" widgets. It adds an AI Usage Dashboard, enhanced camera features like "Stretch," multitasking Pop-up View, and Extra Dark Mode for efficiency.
